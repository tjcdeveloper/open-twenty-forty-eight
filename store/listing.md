## Full description (4000 chars max)

Open Twenty Forty-Eight is a free and open-source take on the classic 2048
puzzle. Swipe to slide the tiles; when two tiles with the same number touch,
they merge into one. Reach 2048 — then keep going.

No ads. No in-app purchases. No tracking. No permissions. Just the game.

FEATURES

• Classic 2048 play with smooth slide, merge, and spawn animations
• Three board sizes: 4×4, 5×5, and 6×6, each with its own best score
• Undo and redo, up to six moves back
• Automatic save — close the app mid-game and pick up where you left off
• Light theme and a true-black dark theme that saves power on OLED screens,
  with a System option that follows your device setting
• Designed for foldables: a compact layout on the cover screen and a
  side-by-side layout on the inner screen, switching live as you fold and
  unfold
• Works fully offline

FREE AND OPEN SOURCE

The complete source code is available on GitHub. Contributions, bug reports,
and suggestions are welcome:
https://github.com/tjcdeveloper/open-twenty-forty-eight
