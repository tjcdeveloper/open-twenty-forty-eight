# Privacy Policy — Open Twenty Forty-Eight

_Last updated: 3 July 2026_

Open Twenty Forty-Eight is a free, open-source game published by tjcdeveloper.

## Data collection

This app collects **no data**. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected, stored, or shared.
- No analytics, advertising, or tracking libraries are included.
- The app requests no permissions and never accesses the network.
- Game state (current board, scores, and settings) is stored only on your
  device and is deleted when the app is uninstalled.

## Third parties

No data is shared with third parties, because no data ever leaves your device.

## Children

The app is suitable for all ages and, as above, collects no data from anyone.

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised as an issue on the project
repository: https://github.com/tjcdeveloper/open-twenty-forty-eight

## Changes

Any future change to this policy will be published in this file with an
updated date.
